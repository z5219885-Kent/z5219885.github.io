AFTER BEFORE BEFORE BEFORE
A world fractured by memory and time

CHAPTER 1 - THE APARTMENT

Kai gets home after a long shift and drops into the chair by his window. The low murmur of the city seeps in, broken by the glow of fractured neon outside. Half-formed faces flicker at the edge of his mind, like static he can’t tune out. A wall-mounted screen stutters to life — the government’s broadcast, all calm tones and empty reassurances about the AfterBefore protocol. “Peace, stability… at what cost?” he mutters, thumb brushing the frayed edges of an old photograph. The faces in it don’t quite belong to him anymore.

CHAPTER 2 - THE BATHROOM

Kai walks into his dim, cold bathroom. The cracked mirror reflects a fragmented self, steam slowly fading as memories slip away. Shards of his identity scatter like broken glass, whispering fractured thoughts of a past he can’t grasp and a future that feels distant.

CHAPTER 3 - THE ENVELOPE

Kai walks out of the bathroom and heads towards bed. The envelope catches his eye, resting on the kitchen table. It’s been bugging him for days—unopened, silent, but heavy with unspoken threats. Curiosity tugs at him, but a warning lingers in his mind: opening it could spill nothing but trouble.

CHAPTER 10 - HOPE

Kai stands alone atop a windswept hill, the cool air brushing against his skin. Below him stretches the sprawling cityscape — a labyrinth of towering skyscrapers, flickering neon lights, and endless shadows. The hum of distant machines and muted voices drift upward, blending into a chaotic symphony of a world on the brink. His eyes scan the horizon, heavy with determination and uncertainty. How can he unravel the web of control that binds this place? How will he find his parents lost somewhere within this fractured maze? A storm brews in his heart, but Kai knows this moment is the start — A fragile of spark of hope...
